# PolyLab Header Concepts

## Recommended order

1. `product-first.png`
   - Best default account header.
   - Fastest to understand for new visitors.
   - Strong fit if the goal is "what is this product?" clarity.

2. `smart-money-signal.png`
   - Best smart-money-specific direction.
   - Keeps the full product context while adding a sharper insight angle.
   - Strong fit if the account will post wallet/holder research often.

3. `smart-money-scoreboard.png`
   - Best if you want a bolder editorial stance on smart money.
   - More opinionated and more "signal product" than "full scanner product."

4. `trust-first.png`
   - Best if the priority is credibility and professional tone.
   - Slightly less immediate than the product-first direction.

5. `brand-minimal.png`
   - Best for cleaner brand presence.
   - Weakest for first-time product understanding.

## Smart money message angles

- `Follow conviction, not noise`
  - Good when positioning smart money as a decision shortcut.

- `See where profitable wallets are already positioned`
  - Good when the emphasis is wallet quality and holder direction.

- `Separate consensus from real wallet conviction`
  - Good when the emphasis is disagreement between price and strong holders.

- `Use smart money to narrow the watchlist`
  - Good when positioning smart money as part of a broader scanner workflow.

## Suggested next step

Choose one general-purpose header and one smart-money-specific header.
Then do a final polish pass on copy, spacing, and screenshot crop rather than creating many more directions.
