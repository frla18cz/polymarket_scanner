It's a high-performance scanner that enriches Gamma API data with calculated metrics:
- **Real-time APR (Win):** Annualized returns based on time to expiry.
- **Spread & Liquidity Analysis:** Quickly find the tightest markets.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Search by category, probability range, and volume.

**Why it matters:**
Many "safe" high-probability outcomes or high-yield long shots are buried in the UI. PolyLab surface these in seconds.
